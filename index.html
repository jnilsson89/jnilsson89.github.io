ForeWind — Golf Shot Calculator
Production app · Android

ForeWind calculates exactly how wind affects a golf shot by combining real-time weather data with the device's compass and GPS. Enter your target distance, point your phone at the flag, and the app tells you how much to adjust — no mental maths required on the course.

Built end-to-end in .NET MAUI, ForeWind is my most complete shipped product to date. Taking it from first commit to the Play Store reinforced every part of the mobile development lifecycle: sensor integration, weather API consumption, offline handling, store compliance, and iterating on real user feedback.
